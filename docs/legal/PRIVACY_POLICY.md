## 3. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information. Fields marked **(sensitive)** receive additional protection.

### 3.1 Information you provide

- **Account & identity:** first name, last name, email address, date of birth, gender (optional), profile photo, bio, country, locale, timezone, and — if you use it — a pet profile (name, species, breed, age, photo) and profile links.
- **Authentication (sensitive):** your password (stored only as a one-way **bcrypt** hash — we never see or store your plaintext password) or, if you sign in with Google, a Google account identifier; one-time passcodes (OTPs) are stored only as short-lived hashes.
- **Contact details:** phone number and WhatsApp number (with country codes), and email/phone attached to saved addresses.
- **Addresses (sensitive where combined):** saved delivery/billing addresses (recipient name, phone, email, address lines, landmark, city, state, PIN code, country).
- **Payments & financial (sensitive):** billing name/email/phone, billing address, **GSTIN** (if you provide one), transaction amounts, coupon codes, and — for hosts/partners requesting payouts — **bank account holder name, account number, IFSC code and/or UPI ID**. **We do not store your full card, CVV or UPI PIN**; card/UPI payment credentials are handled directly by our payment gateway (see Section 8).
- **Identity & KYC documents (sensitive) — hosts, venue partners and brand partners only:** government identifiers and documents such as **Aadhaar number, PAN, passport/ID photo, police-verification document, GST certificate, trade licence and owner-ID uploads**, together with owner name, date of birth, address and bank details, submitted for verification and onboarding.
- **User-generated content:** posts and stories (images/video, captions), pod ideas, comments, chat messages in pods and support, product and club reviews and ratings, uploaded images/videos/reels, and any content you submit to forms (contact, support, host applications, job applications including résumé/portfolio, surveys, FAQs, newsletter sign-ups).
- **Safety / SOS (sensitive):** if you use the in-event safety ("bouncer") feature, we collect your **precise device location (latitude, longitude, accuracy)** and contact phone at the time of an alert, to enable a safety response.

### 3.2 Information generated by your use of the Platform

- **Activity & engagement:** pods you join, host, save or follow; clubs and people you follow; interests; badges, challenges and referrals; likes, comments, and story views; membership, booking and back-out history.
- **Communications:** support chat and ticket threads (including attachments), notifications you receive, and — for CRM/outreach — records of calls and messages, which may include **call recordings and transcripts** where a call is placed through our systems.
- **Device & delivery data:** a device identifier and push-notification tokens (Expo push token / Web Push subscription) so we can deliver notifications; app/OS/platform, page/route, and interaction events (page views, clicks, impressions) for product analytics.
- **Diagnostics & logs:** error and diagnostic logs that may include the page/URL, host, component, and error details. We do not intentionally capture your IP address or raw user-agent in application logs, though diagnostic payloads and stack traces could incidentally contain such data.

### 3.3 Information from third parties

- **Google Sign-In:** if you sign in with Google, we receive your email, name, profile picture, a stable Google identifier and email-verification status.
- **Payment, delivery and communication partners:** confirmations, status updates, tracking information and webhooks (see Section 8).

## 4. How we use your information (purposes)

We use your information to:

1. **Provide the Platform** — create and secure your account, authenticate you, run pods/clubs/bookings, deliver chat, notifications and content, and operate host/venue/brand and admin consoles.
2. **Process payments, payouts and orders** — take payments, calculate fees/commissions, issue invoices, process refunds and back-outs, pay out hosts/partners, and fulfil and ship product orders.
3. **Verify identity and eligibility** — onboard and verify hosts, venue and brand partners (KYC), and enforce eligibility (including age requirements — see Section 12).
4. **Safety, moderation and integrity** — automatically screen content (text and images) for policy violations, maintain account-health and audit records, prevent fraud and abuse, and respond to safety/SOS alerts.
5. **Communicate with you** — send transactional messages (OTPs, confirmations, invoices, receipts, service and security notices) and, where permitted, product and marketing updates you can opt out of.
6. **Analyse and improve** — understand usage, diagnose and fix problems, and develop new features.
7. **Comply with law** — meet tax, accounting, KYC/AML and other legal obligations, and respond to lawful requests.

To keep the community safe, pod and product content you submit (title, description, details, hashtags and up to a few associated images) may be automatically screened by an AI moderation service. This may result in content being blocked, and repeated violations may affect your account health or lead to restrictions. ## 7. How we share information

We do **not** sell your personal data. We share it only:

- **With service providers / sub-processors** who process data on our behalf under contract (Section 8).
- **Within the community, as you direct** — e.g., your public profile, posts, pod participation, reviews and chat messages are visible to other users according to your profile visibility and the nature of the feature. Your bank/KYC/payment details are **never** shown to other users.
- **With hosts, venue and brand partners** to the extent necessary to fulfil a booking or order you make (e.g., a partner receives the delivery details needed to ship your order).
- **For legal reasons** — to comply with law, enforce our Terms, protect the rights, safety and property of Duncit, our users or the public, or in connection with a merger, acquisition or asset sale (with notice where required).

## 10. How long we keep information (retention)

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes above, or as required by law. In particular:

- **Account data** is retained while your account is active. On deletion, see Section 11.
- **Payment, invoice, GST and payout records** are retained for the period required by Indian tax and accounting law (typically up to **8 years**), even after account deletion, in order to meet our legal obligations.
- **KYC / identity documents** (hosts and partners) are retained for the duration of the partnership and any statutory record-keeping period, then deleted or anonymised.
- **Stories** auto-expire after ~24 hours. **Diagnostic/telemetry logs** are retained for a limited, configurable window (currently ~30 days, capped at 90 days). **Uptime/status records** are retained ~90 days. **Draft pods** are cleaned up after a few days.
- **Support, moderation, safety and audit records** are retained as long as needed for integrity, dispute resolution and legal defence.
